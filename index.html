Willy Quach

Email: willy dot quach at weizmann dot ac dot il

I am a Postdoctoral Fellow at the Weizmann Institute of Science, hosted by Zvika Brakerski. I obtained my Ph.D. at Northeastern University, where I was advised by Daniel Wichs.
I'm broadly interested in the foundations of cryptography.
